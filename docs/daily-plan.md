# Daily Plan for a Two-Hour Session

## Suggested Structure

### 1. Opening and framing (10 minutes)
- The lead facilitator introduces the day's topic.
- The faculty member chooses the focus area for the session.

### 2. Self-study phase (35-40 minutes)
- Watch a short instructional video, read a blog, or review a relevant article.
- Capture the main takeaways in notes.

### 3. Creation phase (45-50 minutes)
- Draft or revise a teaching artifact such as a syllabus section, activity, quiz, or lab plan.
- Use the formative evaluator to improve the design.

### 4. Assimilation quiz (15 minutes)
- Complete a personalized quiz shaped by the day's feedback and challenges.

### 5. Reflection and logging (10 minutes)
- Review progress.
- Record what was learned, what remains uncertain, and what to revisit tomorrow.
